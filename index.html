Working method

How I work with AI.

I use AI to compress the distance between idea and artefact, so more time goes to thinking, less to production.

I design with AI at the top of the stack, not as a shortcut, but as a thinking tool. Claude for research synthesis, writing, and stress-testing my own assumptions. Cursor & Replit for prototyping flows engineers can fork and ship without a handoff meeting. Figma + AI for components, documentation, and the parts of design system work that used to take a week.

The output isn't faster moodboards. It's faster decisions, which is the only thing that moves enterprise products forward.

Core beliefs

What I believe about product design.

Four convictions that shape every engagement, earned by being wrong first.

B · 01

Clarity beats cleverness.

Enterprise users don't have time to decode a clever UI. If the right action isn't obvious in 3 seconds, the design has failed, regardless of how good it looks.

B · 02

Systems before screens.

By the time I open Figma, the architecture is decided. A poorly structured IA can't be fixed with better visuals. Pixels are always downstream of decisions.

B · 03

Adoption is the salt KPI.

Everything, from onboarding to error states, is designed to reduce the cost of the first habit. A product no one opens is a portfolio piece, not a product.

B · 04

Process is product.

How a design gets made shapes what it becomes. I document, systemize, and write down what worked. The next designer should ship faster because I was here.
