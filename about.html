Principles

How I think about the work.

Six things I’ll mostly defend, even when it’s inconvenient.

P · 01

Strategy first, screens last.

By the time I open Figma, the architecture is decided. Pixels are downstream of decisions.

P · 02

Audit before redesign.

You can’t fix what you haven’t named. A scenario-driven audit beats a moodboard every time.

P · 03

Defaults are decisions.

The fastest path is the one you don’t make users walk. Opinionated defaults are a craft, not laziness.

P · 04

Compliance is a design surface.

SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO: these aren’t engineering checklists. They shape what users can do, when, and how.

P · 05

Adoption > aesthetic.

A product no one opens is a portfolio piece, not a product. Solve adoption first, beauty second.

P · 06

Leave teams better than I found them.

I document, I systemize, I write down what worked. The next designer should ship faster because I was here.
